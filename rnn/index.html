Latent Variable

Latent, shared, random variable can account for large amount of variance in V1 (Ecker et al 201400104-4))

RNN's which have a Latent Random Variable at each time step parameterized by the prior hidden layer (ht-1) work better than vanilla RNN's for highly structured sequence prediction (J .Chung et al 2015)
